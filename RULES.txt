RULES OF THE GAME

1. SPY
    - if bull we go long
    - price has to be above MA50 and MA200
    - we want to go with momentum. 
2. look at sector trend
    - only buy when sector trend is bull
3. look at stock trend
    - look at individual stocks
    - only buy sector leaders
    - only buy when the current (or last closing price) hasn't overextended (under 5%).
4. RSI/other
    - Nice to have, may make this stratergy more complicated. 
    - RSI between 25%-75%
5. STOP LOSS/ SELL LIMIT
    - Have stop loss at 5% of the average price.
    - Limit order of 10-15%. 
    - risk-reward ratio of 1:2 or 1:3
